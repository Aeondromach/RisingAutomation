The Impact of Convoy

Convoy™ will inevitably and irreversibly shake the economy. But how exactly will it shift?

Overall, Convoy™ will have both good and bad impacts over the world, but let's explore each of these consequences, so that you can decide for yourself which path is the best.

Job Market People will lose their jobs, especislly truck drivers. Convoy™ will lead to around 1-2 million driver jobs lost to AI. Convoy™ has the oppurtunity to open new, higher paying jobs, such as Programmers, Engineers, and Testers.
Economy With the changes in qualifications needed in the transportation industry, more and more people will be put into poverty unless new jobs are opened up.
