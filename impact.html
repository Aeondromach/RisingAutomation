The Impact of Convoy™

Convoy™ will inevitably and irreversibly shake the economy. But how exactly will it shift?

Overall, Convoy™ will have both good and bad impacts over the world, but let's explore each of these consequences so that you can decide for yourself which path is the best.

Differences in the Job Market People will lose their jobs, especially truck drivers. Convoy™ will lead to around 1-2 million driver jobs lost. Convoy™ has the opportunity to open new, higher paying jobs, such as Programmers, Engineers, and Testers.
Differences in the Economy With the changes in qualifications needed in the transportation industry, more and more people will be put into poverty unless new jobs are opened up. With virtually 0 labor costs, less money spent on fuel, drivers with no breaks, and less likely crashes, Convoy™ will not only reduce prices in the market, but also make them more guaranteed to be delivered intact.
Differences in the Environment Convoy™ vehicles trucks are made with Lithium batteries, which utilize Cobalt and Lithium.

Both resources are extremely rare and bad for the environment, especially Lithium, which is typically trashed after the battery degrades.		 Convoy™ vehicles use mounted solar panels to charge as they drive, as well as the docking stations that also charge them.

The docking stations are powered by solar and wind, that energy being funneled into station batteries to avoid wasting energy.
